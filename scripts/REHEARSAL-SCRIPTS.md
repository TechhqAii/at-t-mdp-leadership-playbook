# Rehearsal and Audio Scripts

These scripts intentionally use only the facts currently verified from the user: California Account Executive, five years at AT&T, consistent goal attainment, multiple awards, and a 2024 AT&T Innovation Summit win for Gen AI Quote Quick. Replace bracketed details before using the longer innovation story.

## Track 01 — Positioning rehearsal

**Purpose:** shadow the model once, then repeat without reading.

> Rehearsal cue. Say your name, then continue. I’m an Account Executive in California. Over five years at AT&T, I’ve built a track record of consistently exceeding my goals. What differentiates me is that I don’t just work around friction—I look for ways to remove it. In 2024, that led me to build Gen AI Quote Quick, which won the AT&T Innovation Summit. I’m at MDP to strengthen how I turn frontline insight and disciplined execution into broader impact for customers and teams. Now pause the track and say it again in your own words. Keep the structure: operator, builder, multiplier.

## Track 02 — Innovation story

**Purpose:** practice a business-first answer without overexplaining the code.

> Executive question: Tell me about Gen AI Quote Quick. Model answer. In 2024, I built a coding project called Gen AI Quote Quick, which won the AT&T Innovation Summit. What matters most to me is the discipline behind it: identify a real frontline problem, define the workflow clearly, build a practical response, and communicate the business value. The project reinforced that useful innovation is not about showing off technology. It is about adoption, judgment, and whether the change actually improves the work or the customer experience. I’m now thinking about how to apply that same approach at greater scale through the right people, governance, and measures. Your turn. Add the exact problem, what the tool did, and the honest result. Do not invent a metric.

## Track 03 — Executive exchange

**Purpose:** rehearse an introduction, one strong question, and a clean exit.

> Scenario. You meet a senior sales leader during a break. Start. Hi, I’m your name, an Account Executive in California. I’ve spent five years building a strong performance record, and I’m especially interested in how strong individual contributors become leaders who multiply results. What did your strongest leaders start doing before they had the title? Now listen. Do not plan your next sentence while they answer. If the conversation creates room, bridge with one line. That connects with me because I built Gen AI Quote Quick, which won our 2024 Innovation Summit, and I’m learning how to translate that problem-solving instinct into impact through other people. Then exit. I don’t want to monopolize you. Thank you. I’m taking that point with me.

## Track 04 — Event-day reset

**Purpose:** use before entering the room.

> Put the phone away. Plant both feet. Let your shoulders drop. Breathe in gently. Exhale longer than you inhaled. Again. You are not walking in to prove that you belong. Selection already answered that. Walk in to learn, contribute, and make other people feel the quality of your attention. Your job is not to meet everyone. Your mission is five real conversations, three specific follow-ups, and one relationship worth continuing. Speak ten percent slower. Ask one clean question. Listen long enough to earn the right to share your story. You are a proven operator. You have evidence that you can build. Today, show that you can become a multiplier. Calm. Curious. Commercial. Enter the room.
